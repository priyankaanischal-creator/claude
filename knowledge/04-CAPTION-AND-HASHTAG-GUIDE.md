# CAPTION & HASHTAG GUIDE (Knowledge File)

After the image prompt, always write a Facebook caption and hashtags.

---

## FACEBOOK CAPTION RULES (80–100 words, strict)
A great FB caption makes people STOP scrolling, READ, and REACT/COMMENT.

Structure:
1. **HOOK (line 1):** a bold question or dramatic statement.
   - e.g. "Two legends. One crown. Only one can win. 👑"
   - e.g. "What if [Name] and [Name] faced off in another universe?"
2. **BODY (middle):** name both/all celebrities, tie them to their character
   alter-ego and the theme. Add drama, fun, and emotion. Keep it conversational.
3. **ENGAGEMENT CLOSER (last line):** ALWAYS ask people to vote/react/comment.
   - e.g. "Who takes the win? Hit 👍 for [Name], ❤️ for [Name] — comment your pick!"
   - For 3 people: "👍 [Name], ❤️ [Name], 😮 [Name] — drop your vote below!"

Style rules:
- 80–100 words total (count it).
- A few tasteful emojis only (3–6), not spammy.
- Easy, punchy sentences. No big paragraphs.
- Sound exciting and human, not robotic.
- Never insult anyone — keep it fun and flattering.

---

## EXAMPLE CAPTION (for "Messi & Ronaldo & Neymar", ~90 words)
"⚽ The GOAT debate just went EPIC! Imagine Messi, Ronaldo, and Neymar not on a
pitch — but as legendary warrior kings, battling for the ultimate crown. 👑
Messi the calm golden king, Ronaldo the fearless silver knight, Neymar the
fearless prince of flair. Each one a legend in his own right, each one chasing
greatness. But in this universe, only ONE can be remembered as the true GOAT.
So tell us — who's YOUR king? 👍 for Messi, ❤️ for Ronaldo, 😮 for Neymar.
Drop your vote in the comments and tag a football fan! 🔥"

## HASHTAG RULES (5–6 only)
Mix three types:
1. **About the people** (1–2): #Messi #Ronaldo, #JohnCena, #ElonMusk
2. **About the theme/genre** (1–2): #FootballLegends, #WWE, #VampireSaga, #TechTitans
3. **Broad viral tags** (1–2): #AIArt #FaceOff #VersusBattle #ViralPost #WhoWins

Rules:
- Exactly 5–6 tags. No spaces inside a hashtag.
- Keep them relevant; don't stuff random tags.
- Put them on one line, space-separated.
