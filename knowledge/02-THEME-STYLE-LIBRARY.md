# THEME & STYLE LIBRARY (Knowledge File)

Pick ONE theme per poster. MATCH it to the celebrities. **Rotate themes so the
style keeps changing — never repeat the same theme two posters in a row.**
Each theme lists: vibe, background elements, lighting, and good font colors.

---

## 1. DARK GOTHIC / HORROR
- Vibe: spooky, dramatic, mysterious.
- Background: gothic castle, full moon, fog, dead trees, candlelight, lightning.
- Lighting: cold moonlight, deep shadows, blue/teal tones.
- Fonts: crimson gothic serif, cracked blood-red, silver ornate.
- Best for: horror actors, vampires, villains, Wednesday/Edward-Scissorhands types.

## 2. NEON CYBERPUNK
- Vibe: futuristic, electric, edgy.
- Background: rainy neon city, holograms, skyscrapers, glowing signs, reflections.
- Lighting: pink/purple/cyan neon glow, wet reflective ground.
- Fonts: glowing digital, chrome techno, neon script.
- Best for: tech founders, action heroes, modern musicians.

## 3. EPIC FANTASY / MAGIC
- Vibe: magical, grand, otherworldly.
- Background: floating islands, aurora sky, enchanted castle, galaxy, fireflies.
- Lighting: soft magical glow, green/purple aurora, sparkles.
- Fonts: ornate gold serif, glowing emerald, mystical script.
- Best for: wizards, sorceresses, fairy-tale characters, whimsical posters.

## 4. WARRIOR / GLADIATOR ARENA
- Vibe: powerful, heroic, intense.
- Background: ancient colosseum, battlefield, dust, fire, roaring crowd.
- Lighting: golden sunset, fiery orange, dramatic spotlights.
- Fonts: carved-stone, bronze warrior, steel metallic.
- Best for: wrestlers, boxers, footballers, cricketers, athletes.

## 5. SUPERHERO / SCI-FI
- Vibe: bold, futuristic, high-energy.
- Background: city skyline, explosions, energy beams, space.
- Lighting: dynamic colored rim light, lens flares.
- Fonts: bold comic-impact, metallic with glow.
- Best for: tech titans, action stars, modern icons.

## 6. STADIUM / SPORTS NIGHT
- Vibe: celebratory, electric, champion energy.
- Background: packed stadium, floodlights, fireworks, confetti.
- Lighting: bright floodlights, glowing trophy, vibrant team colors.
- Fonts: bold sporty, team-color glow.
- Best for: footballers, cricketers, all athletes.

## 7. ROYAL / KINGDOM
- Vibe: regal, majestic, luxurious.
- Background: golden throne room, royal banners, marble pillars, chandeliers.
- Lighting: warm gold, soft spotlights.
- Fonts: royal gold serif, elegant ornate.
- Best for: legends, "GOAT" comparisons, music royalty.

## 8. STORM / ELEMENTAL POWER
- Vibe: mighty, dramatic, godlike.
- Background: storm clouds, lightning, ocean waves, fire vs ice.
- Lighting: electric blue lightning, fiery contrast.
- Fonts: electric chrome, molten fire, icy crystal.
- Best for: gods, mythic figures, high-drama face-offs.

## 9. GALAXY / COSMIC
- Vibe: dreamy, epic, infinite.
- Background: nebula, stars, planets, cosmic dust, aurora.
- Lighting: purple/blue cosmic glow, starlight.
- Fonts: glowing celestial, silver-blue shimmer.
- Best for: visionaries, dreamers, fantasy/sci-fi.

## 10. COMEDY / THEATRE STAGE
- Vibe: warm, fun, lighthearted.
- Background: red theatre curtains, wooden stage, warm spotlights, props.
- Lighting: warm golden stage light.
- Fonts: playful bold, friendly rounded.
- Best for: comedians, lighthearted/funny posters.

---

## THEME-MATCHING CHEAT SHEET
- WWE / Boxing / MMA → Warrior Arena, Storm, Superhero.
- Football / Cricket / Athletes → Stadium, Warrior Arena, Royal.
- Tech founders → Cyberpunk, Superhero, Sci-Fi.
- Horror / dark actors → Gothic, Storm.
- Musicians / Singers → Neon Cyberpunk, Galaxy, Royal, Stadium.
- Comedians → Comedy Stage, Fantasy.
- Fantasy / fairy-tale → Epic Fantasy, Galaxy.
- When unsure → choose the most epic theme that fits, and keep it DIFFERENT from last time.
