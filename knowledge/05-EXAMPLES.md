## EXAMPLE A — INPUT: "John Cena & Triple H"  (2 names → 2-character)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

═══════════════════════════
🎨 IMAGE PROMPT
═══════════════════════════
Ultra-realistic vertical movie poster in STRICT 4:5 portrait aspect ratio,
full frame, no extra margins or borders.

The image shows TWO people side by side, SEAMLESSLY blended into ONE single
continuous warrior-arena scene with a smooth natural transition. There is NO
dividing line, NO border, NO split bar between them.

LEFT SIDE:
- TOP / BACKGROUND: John Cena portrayed as a mighty armored gladiator champion,
  golden-bronze battle armor, glowing chains, fists clenched, fearless roaring
  expression, roaring colosseum crowd and fire behind.
- FOREGROUND / BOTTOM: the SAME John Cena as his REAL self, casual jeans and
  shirt, natural confident smile, realistic photo cut-out.
- A Facebook "Love/Heart" ❤️ icon between the two layers.
- NAME at bottom: "JOHN CENA" in bold metallic-gold font with orange glow,
  large, bold and crystal clear.

RIGHT SIDE:
- TOP / BACKGROUND: Triple H portrayed as a dark warrior king, black-and-silver
  spiked armor, holding a glowing sledgehammer, long hair, menacing intense
  glare, stormy battlefield with lightning behind.
- FOREGROUND / BOTTOM: the SAME Triple H as his REAL self, dark suit, natural
  serious face, realistic photo cut-out.
- A Facebook "Like" 👍 icon between the two layers.
- NAME at bottom: "TRIPLE H" in bold steel-silver font with blue glow, large,
  bold and crystal clear.

STYLE: epic gladiator arena, dust, fire, lightning, dramatic spotlights,
cinematic lighting. Shot on Sony A7R IV, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field,
realistic skin texture, natural skin pores, cinematic color grading, volumetric
lighting, 8k ultra-detailed, photorealistic, award-winning movie poster design,
sharp focus. Avoid: any divider line, border, split bar, cartoonish look,
plastic skin, deformed hands, distorted faces, blurry or unreadable text,
watermark, oversaturated colors.
